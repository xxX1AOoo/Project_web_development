About Us

Founded in 2023, FEARLESS is a humble society for student debate.We offer a wide range of services, activities, programmes, facilities and opportunities to help every student make the most of their time at university. As well as supporting over 300 student clubs, USU is responsible for providing lots of fun things beyond lectures and exams, including major festivals, parties, performances and a huge range of student-focused events.

Whether you're a gamer, an actor, a debater or a dancer; a singer, a writer or simply love yoga. Want to challenge a chess champion or quaff a cab sav; looking to learn a new language or taste a new tea.No matter where you come from or what you're into, the University of Adelaide has a club for you.

We also have a great volunteering programme, as well as leadership and paid work opportunities. We continue to provide world-leading events and services for our current and future members.
